Advanced Features for Smarter Cities

Modern administrative tools designed to facilitate clean and swift municipal action.

Geo-Tagged Reporting

Easily upload photos of local civic issues. Automatic GPS tagging ensures exact coordinates are sent to municipal officers.

AI-Powered Classification

Machine learning models analyze uploaded issue details and route them to the relevant department (e.g., PWD, Water Board, Sanitation) instantly.

Transparent SLA Tracking

Track complaints from "Submitted" to "In Progress" and "Resolved". Transparent service level agreements (SLAs) hold authorities accountable.
